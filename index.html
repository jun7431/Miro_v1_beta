Refined main app

Map + itinerary, with V1's chip builder built in

A real travel-planning app. Polished SVG map of Hongdae · Yeonnam on the right, route summary + compact V1-style builder + itinerary + refines on the left, sticky save/share/open-in-map. Conversation is reduced to a small Ask Miro drawer — opt-in, never required.

  • Compact V1 builder lives inside the panel — collapsible, always one click away
  • Map + list stay in sync · clicking a pin highlights the stop and shows a place card
  • Refine chips and Ask Miro both adjust the same route — neither is mandatory
  • Mobile responsive: map on top, panel below, sticky actions
